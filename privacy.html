Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and keep data collection to a minimum. This site does not use tracking cookies. If you submit the contact form, we process your details solely to respond to your enquiry and maintain a short record of the conversation.

  • What we collect: your name, organisation, email address and message content.
  • Why we collect it: to respond to your enquiry and maintain basic records for accountability.
  • How long we keep it: up to 12 months unless a longer period is required for compliance.
  • Your choices: you can request a copy or deletion of your enquiry data at any time using the contact form — please mention “Privacy” in your message.
  • Security: we use reputable third-party form delivery to transmit your message securely to our inbox.
