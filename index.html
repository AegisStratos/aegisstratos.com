PROTECTION THROUGH INNOVATION — applied with purpose, built for resilience.

Aegis Stratos was founded in response to a truth witnessed in Ukraine — that technology saves lives when it is shaped by those who face real threats and developed by those who know how to deliver at scale. As uncrewed systems began to redefine modern warfare, we recognised the need for a company that could unite Ukrainian operational ingenuity with advanced AI-driven engineering and disciplined programme delivery.

This alignment brings together operational credibility, technical precision, and humanitarian intent — defining a new kind of defence company for a new era.

AI NATIVE BY DESIGN

Aegis Stratos is an AI-First company. We build through it, with it, and because of it. Artificial intelligence defines how we innovate, manufacture, and operate — from automated design and digital simulation to predictive logistics and adaptive flight control.

By embedding AI throughout both our aircraft and our organisation, we accelerate development cycles, enhance security, reduce cost, and react to changing threats at unprecedented speed.
